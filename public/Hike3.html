Squaw Peak

Distance: 7 miles

Difficulty: Moderate

Description: Starting at Rock Canyon Park, the trail gains about 2800 feet in a hike that will take about 4 or 5 hours. The view at Squaw Peak gives a spectacular view of Provo.
