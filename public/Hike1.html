Y-Hike

Distance: 1.1 miles

Difficulty: Easy

Description: Starting at the Y-Mountain Trailhead, this hike consists of a set of switchbacks gaining an elevation of 1000 feet. At the top, you get a view of BYU Campus and much of Provo.
