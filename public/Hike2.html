Mount Timpanogos

Distance: 15 miles roundtrip

Difficulty: Difficult

Description: Starting up Provo Canyon, the trail up to the peak is a scenic route passing waterfalls and wildflowers. The summit is at an elevation of almost 12,000 feet.
